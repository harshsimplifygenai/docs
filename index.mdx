## Get started in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [verbeo.ai](https://verbeo.ai) and set up your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Navigate to **Settings → API Keys** in the dashboard to generate your first key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an AI agent">
    Use the dashboard or the [Create Agent API](/api-reference/agents/create) to configure your agent's voice, prompt, and tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first call">
    Use the [Initiate Call API](/api-reference/calls/initiate) or the dashboard to place your first outbound call.
  </Step>
</Steps>
